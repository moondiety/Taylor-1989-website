Discography

1989 (Taylor’s Version) is the fourth of the six re-recorded albums on Taylor Swift’s mission to regain ownership over her catalogue from 2006 to 2017.
It was released on October 27, 2023, the same date as the original album.

1989 is a concept album by Taylor Swift that revolves around the themes of fame, pop culture, and romance.
It was inspired by her experiences of transitioning from country music to pop music and navigating the public eye.
The album features a diverse range of genres, including pop, dance, and electropop, with catchy hooks and relatable lyrics.
